The Creation of Aria

At Aria, we strive to provide a seamless platform for music lovers to explore their favorite artists. Aria was created for a group project of a course I took in my junior year as an application of what we have learned through the semester.

The goal of Aria is how a simple database can be constructed and created to enhance the user's experience, to create an organized, seamless, personal, and easy-to-use application.
